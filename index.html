Discover the Power of Trezór Hardware Wallet

Next-Gen Security

Experience unparalleled protection with Trezór's advanced cryptographic technology. Our hardware wallet keeps your private keys completely offline in cold storage, creating an impenetrable barrier against online threats. With military-grade encryption and secure chip technology, your digital assets remain safe from hackers, malware, and cyber attacks.

Cutting-Edge Architecture

At the core of Trezór lies a sophisticated security architecture. The dedicated secure chip performs all cryptographic operations internally, ensuring your private keys never leave the protected environment. Enhanced with PIN protection and optional passphrase security, Trezór creates multiple layers of defense against both physical and digital threats.

Multi-Coin Support

Manage your entire crypto portfolio with support for over 1,000 digital assets. From Bitcoin and Ethereum to the latest DeFi tokens, Trezór is your universal key to the crypto ecosystem. Regular firmware updates ensure continuous compatibility with new cryptocurrencies while maintaining the highest security standards for your existing assets.

Intuitive Experience

Trezór combines robust security with user-friendly design. The sleek Trezór Suite software provides a seamless desktop experience, while the device's clear display and simple controls make transaction verification effortless. Perfect for both crypto newcomers and experienced investors, Trezór makes digital asset management accessible without compromising security.

Foolproof Recovery

Never worry about losing access to your funds. Trezór's comprehensive backup system uses a 24-word recovery seed based on BIP39 standards. This universal backup ensures you can restore your entire wallet on any device, even if your original Trezór is lost or damaged. Our recovery process maintains the highest security standards while remaining simple and straightforward.
